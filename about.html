Who We Are

Six Sigma Inc. Pvt. Ltd. is an expert consulting and solution provider company established in the year 2016 focusing on the current technologies to help boost the productivity of communities, government, businesses, institutions and corporate houses in Nepal. Six Sigma Inc. comprises of professional engineers, consultants, academicians, trainers and businessmen. We at Six Sigma Inc. are committed to providing the best in class services with higher concentration on Computer Information, Communication & Broadcasting Technologies. Focused on Computer Information, Communication & Broadcasting Technologies, we are committed to providing best-in-class solutions. By staying up-to-date with the latest technological advancements, we ensure our clients receive innovative and customized strategies. Through strategic planning and practical guidance, we help organizations optimize their operations and achieve their goals.

Talented people, working together
